Before You Begin

Follow these steps for the most accurate results

💻
Use a laptop or desktop

Mobile screens vary in color accuracy and size, which may affect your results.

☀️
Maximum screen brightness

Set your display brightness to 100% and reduce any ambient glare on the screen.

👁️
Test one eye at a time

Cover the other eye with your hand. Wearing glasses or contacts is fine.

📏
Correct viewing distance

Sit approximately 50–70 cm (20–28 inches) from your screen.
