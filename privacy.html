Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2024

1. Overview

Color Vision Test ("we", "us", or "our") is a free educational tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact details unless you voluntarily provide them via the optional email report form.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or browser fingerprints.
  • We do not transmit your test responses or results anywhere.

3. Local Storage

Your most recent test result (type, severity, and date) is saved using your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Remains exclusively on your device.
  • Is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Does not include personally identifiable information.
  • Can be deleted at any time via the "Clear My Data" link in the footer.

4. Optional Email Submission

If you choose to submit your email address to receive a detailed report, that email is transmitted to and processed by Formspree. Their privacy policy governs how that data is stored and handled. We will never sell, share, or rent your email address. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email we send.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as pages visited, time spent on site, general geographic location (country/city level), device type and browser, and how you found our website (search engine, social media, etc.).

No personally identifiable information is collected. Google Analytics uses cookies for this purpose.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's privacy policy.

6. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

For more information, see Google's privacy policy.

7. Google Fonts

This site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

8. Children

This tool is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes take effect when posted on this page.

10. Affiliate Disclosure

This website participates in affiliate advertising programs. Some links to third-party products (such as color-correcting glasses) are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through them — at no additional cost to you.

We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

Affiliate relationships do not influence our test methodology, editorial content, or the products we choose to feature.
